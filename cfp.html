Call for Papers

The EGAI 2025 invites submissions of original research papers on all aspects of Evolutionary Game Theory and Artificial Intelligence. We encourage submissions that explore the synergies between these two fields, addressing both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

Topics

Relevant topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Evolutionary game dynamics in AI
  • Learning in games
  • Multi-agent learning
  • Reinforcement learning in game-theoretic settings
  • Algorithmic game theory and complexity
  • Mechanism design for AI systems
  • Fairness, privacy, and ethics in AI games
  • Game theory for multi-robot systems
  • Evolutionary computation for game solving
  • Empirical studies of game theory in AI applications

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: September 15, 2024
  • Full Paper Submission Deadline: October 1, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: November 15, 2024
  • Camera-Ready Submission: December 1, 2024
