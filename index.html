About EGAI 2025

The International Conference on Evolutionary Game and Artificial Intelligence (EGAI 2025) is a premier forum for researchers and practitioners to share their latest findings in the intersection of evolutionary game theory and artificial intelligence. Hosted in a vibrant city, EGAI 2025 aims to foster discussions on theoretical advancements, innovative algorithms, and practical applications. Read more...
